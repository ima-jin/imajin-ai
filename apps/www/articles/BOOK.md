## Notes

**The pattern:** Each part of the save series (chapters 15-20) follows the same structure ‚Äî name the extraction, show the real value underneath, demonstrate how the trust graph and .fair chain redirect the money to the humans who created the value. The repetition is intentional. By chapter 20 the reader has seen the same disease and the same cure six times. The protocol becomes obvious.

**The interstitials:** Human stories between the systems chapters. The book breathes. Systems ‚Üí human ‚Üí systems ‚Üí human ‚Üí close.

**The appendices:** Living documents. Updated as the field evolves. Community-contributed. The book stays alive after publication.

**The invitation:** The book ends with a return address. Not a website. Not a QR code. An email. A human on the other end. The whole thesis in one gesture.
