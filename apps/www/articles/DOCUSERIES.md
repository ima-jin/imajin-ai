## Production Notes

**Tone:** Not TED Talk. Not YouTube explainer. Think Anthony Bourdain meets Nardwuar. Personal, funny, irreverent, but underneath it â€” dead serious about the thesis.

**Visual language:** Real locations. Real people. No stock footage. No motion graphics explaining blockchains. If you can't show it with a person in a room, it doesn't belong in the show.

**Music:** Original. Attributed through .fair. Every musician in the chain gets compensated through the protocol the show is about.

**Length:** 2 minutes per episode. Tight. No filler. The essays already did the explaining â€” the show does the feeling.

**Attribution:** Every contributor is in the .fair chain. Writer, director, editor, actor, musician, animator, colorist. The credits aren't at the end â€” they're in the manifest. Verifiable. Permanent. Compensated.

**Distribution:** First on imajin.ai. Then wherever it travels, the .fair manifest travels with it. The show cannot be extracted from its attribution chain without breaking the protocol it's demonstrating.
